# Pipeline

Master pipeline of all active roles. Updated by the job-search-agent and manually by you.

## Ranking
- **1** — dream role, pursue aggressively
- **2** — strong fit, actively pursue
- **3** — worth exploring, research first
- **4** — lower priority, keep on radar

## Status Definitions
- **To Research** — not yet vetted
- **Networking** — outreach in progress
- **Ready to Apply** — worth applying now
- **Applied** — application submitted
- **Interviewing** — active interview process
- **Hold** — paused (agent skips this role)
- **Rejected** — received rejection
- **Closed** — role disappeared or no longer relevant
